# Wizard's League

**A roguelite wizard battle RPG — fight your way through 8 elemental gyms and prove you're the greatest spellcaster alive.**

🎮 **Play now:** [https://jonahbyu.github.io/Wizard-league/](https://jonahbyu.github.io/Wizard-league/)

---

## What is it?

Wizard's League drops you into a dark fantasy world where magic is a competitive sport. You pick an element, build your spellbook, and battle through increasingly brutal wizard opponents — Emberweaves, Glacialmancers, Thunderscribes, Voidweavers, and more — all leading up to an elemental gym boss.

Every run is different. Every death teaches you something new.

---

## Features

- **8 Elements** — Fire, Water, Ice, Lightning, Earth, Nature, Plasma, and Air. Each plays completely differently with its own spell mechanics and status effects.
- **Build your spellbook** — choose spells after each battle, swap out weak ones, and craft a combo that snowballs into something terrifying.
- **Turn-based action queue** — plan your moves, manage cooldowns, and time your spells to chain devastating combos.
- **Passives & Artifacts** — unlock powerful passive abilities and artifacts that persist between runs, making each playthrough stronger than the last.
- **3 Wizard classes** — Battlemage, Hexblade, or Arcanist. Each has a unique stat profile and starting boon.
- **Full wizard customization** — hat color, outfit, beard, staff wood, orb glow, eye color. Look as dangerous as you fight.
- **Rival system** — a rival wizard named Ash shows up to ruin your day at the worst possible moment.
- **Campfires, shops, gyms** — rest to recover HP, spend gold on upgrades, or challenge the gym boss for a passive ability reward.
- **3 save slots** — run multiple playthroughs at once.

---

## How to Play

1. Pick a save slot and name your wizard
2. Customize your wizard's look
3. Choose your element
4. Fight your way through the map — pick your battles, rest when you need to, and shop wisely
5. Defeat all 8 gym bosses to become the champion

No installs. No downloads. Runs entirely in your browser.
